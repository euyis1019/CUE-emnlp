% Section 3: The Internal Lifecycle of Code Reasoning
\section{The Internal Lifecycle of Code Reasoning}
\label{sec:brewing}

\begin{figure*}[t]
\centering
\includegraphics[width=\textwidth]{resolution_taxonomy.png}
\caption{Representative layer-wise trajectories from the anchor setting (Qwen2.5-Coder-7B), illustrated on Computing samples. Probing becomes correct first (FPCL), CSD catches up later (FJC), and trajectories then diverge into four resolution outcomes: Resolved, Overprocessed, Misresolved, and Unresolved.}
\label{fig:resolution_taxonomy}
\end{figure*}

% 中文: 对每个样本 $x$（source prompt $S_x$，ground-truth $t_x^*$），模型的一次前向传播产生一串随层深演化的 hidden states；$\Phi_{\mathrm{P}}^\ell$ 与 $\Phi_{\mathrm{C}}^\ell$ 在每一层各给出一个诊断分布，于是代码推理变成一条可沿深度直接观察的内部轨迹。
For each sample $x$ (source prompt $S_x$, ground-truth answer $t_x^*$), a single forward pass produces a sequence of hidden states $\{\mathbf{h}_x^\ell\}_{\ell=0}^{L-1}$ that evolve across layers; $\Phi_{\mathrm{P}}^\ell$ and $\Phi_{\mathrm{C}}^\ell$ each yield a diagnostic distribution at every layer, turning code reasoning into an internal trajectory that can be directly observed along depth.
%
% 中文: Figure~\ref{fig:resolution_taxonomy} 展示了 anchor setting 上的代表性轨迹：probing 首先变得正确，而 CSD 在若干层之后才跟上。答案信息先以外部可读的形式出现，随后才转化为模型自身可解码的状态。
\cref{fig:resolution_taxonomy} displays representative trajectories from the anchor setting: probing becomes correct first, and CSD catches up only several layers later. Answer information surfaces in an externally readable form before it is converted into a state that the model itself can decode.

% 中文: 记 $\hat{t}_{\mathrm{P}}^\ell$, $\hat{t}_{\mathrm{C}}^\ell$, $\hat{t}_{\mathrm{out}}$ 的定义。在最早的若干层（大约前 [XX]\%），两个诊断函数都处于高熵状态，概率质量分散在多个 digit 和 $\bar{d}$ 之间——模型在变换表征，但答案相关结构尚未在任一视角下浮现。这个 pre-brewing 区域的结束标志是 probing 首次正确的那一层。
Let $\hat{t}_{\mathrm{P}}^\ell \triangleq \arg\max_{t \in \mathcal{T}} \Phi_{\mathrm{P}}^\ell(\mathbf{h}_x^\ell)[t]$, $\hat{t}_{\mathrm{C}}^\ell \triangleq \arg\max_{t \in \mathcal{T}} \Phi_{\mathrm{C}}^\ell(\mathbf{h}_x^\ell)[t]$, and $\hat{t}_{\mathrm{out}} \triangleq \mathcal{M}(S_x)$ denote the model's final output. In the earliest layers (roughly the first 20\%), both diagnostic functions remain high-entropy, with probability mass dispersed across multiple digits and the non-digit class $\bar{d}$---the model is transforming its representations, yet answer-relevant structure has not emerged under either diagnostic view. This \emph{pre-brewing} region ends at the layer where probing first becomes correct. We define
%
\begin{align}
\label{eq:fpcl}
\mathrm{FPCL}(S_x) &\triangleq \min\{\ell : \hat{t}_{\mathrm{P}}^\ell = t_x^*\}, \\
\label{eq:fjc}
\mathrm{FJC}(S_x) &\triangleq \min\{\ell : \hat{t}_{\mathrm{P}}^\ell = t_x^* \;\land\; \hat{t}_{\mathrm{C}}^\ell = t_x^*\}.
\end{align}

% 中文: 在 anchor setting 上，FPCL 平均出现在归一化深度 [XX]\%，FJC 出现在 [XX]\%；这一先后关系在全部实验中均成立。我们将间隔称为 brewing——答案已经存在，但尚未变成模型自己可解码的形式。其长度平均为 [XX] 层（总深度的 [XX]\%），说明这段中间过程并非边缘现象。
In the anchor setting, FPCL occurs at mean normalized depth 20\% and FJC at 72\%; the ordering $\mathrm{FPCL}(S_x) < \mathrm{FJC}(S_x)$ holds across all experimental configurations. We call the interval $[\mathrm{FPCL},\,\mathrm{FJC})$ \textbf{brewing}---the answer already exists, but has not yet been converted into a form the model itself can decode. Its length,
%
\begin{equation}
\label{eq:brewing_duration}
\Delta_{\mathrm{brew}}(S_x) = \mathrm{FJC}(S_x) - \mathrm{FPCL}(S_x),
\end{equation}
%
% 中文: 那么一旦到达（或未能到达）FJC，后续轨迹是否统一？
averages 14.5 layers (52\% of total depth), confirming that this intermediate process is far from marginal. Does the trajectory become uniform once FJC is reached---or not reached?

% 中文: 答案是否定的。Figure~\ref{fig:resolution_taxonomy} 示意了 brewing 过程及其后续分化的四种 resolution outcome。
The answer is no. \cref{fig:resolution_taxonomy} illustrates the brewing process and the four \textbf{resolution outcomes} into which trajectories subsequently diverge. Let $\bar{\Phi}_{\mathcal{W}}(t) \triangleq \frac{1}{|\mathcal{W}|}\sum_{\ell \in \mathcal{W}} \Phi_{\mathrm{C}}^\ell(\mathbf{h}^\ell)[t]$ denote the average CSD probability assigned to digit $t$ over a tail window $\mathcal{W} = \{\ell \mid \ell \geq \lfloor 3L/4 \rfloor\}$. The four outcome categories are defined as follows:
%
\begin{equation}
\label{eq:outcome_cases}
\resizebox{0.91\columnwidth}{!}{$\displaystyle
\mathrm{Outcome}(S_x) = \begin{cases}
\textbf{Resolved} & \mathrm{FJC} \neq \varnothing \;\land\; \hat{t}_{\mathrm{out}} = t_x^* \\[3pt]
\textbf{Overprocessed} & \mathrm{FJC} \neq \varnothing \;\land\; \hat{t}_{\mathrm{out}} \neq t_x^* \\[3pt]
\textbf{Misresolved} & \mathrm{FJC} = \varnothing \;\land\; \max_{t \in \mathcal{D}}\, \bar{\Phi}_{\mathcal{W}}(t) \geq \tfrac{1}{2} \\[3pt]
\textbf{Unresolved} & \mathrm{FJC} = \varnothing \;\land\; \max_{t \in \mathcal{D}}\, \bar{\Phi}_{\mathcal{W}}(t) < \tfrac{1}{2}
\end{cases}
$}
\end{equation}

% 中文: 直觉上，Resolved 与 Overprocessed 的区分在于：模型曾在某层同时通过两个诊断，但后续层是否保住了正确答案。Overprocessed 意味着正确计算已经形成却被后续层破坏。Misresolved 与 Unresolved 则刻画两种不同的失败：前者收敛到了稳定但错误的答案，后者在现有深度内始终未形成稳定 resolution。
Intuitively, Resolved and Overprocessed are distinguished by whether subsequent layers preserve the correct answer after it has been jointly attested by both diagnostics: Overprocessed means that a correct computation was formed yet destroyed by later layers. Misresolved and Unresolved characterize two distinct failure modes: the former converges to a stable but incorrect answer, while the latter never reaches a stable resolution within the available depth.
%
% 中文: 值得注意的是，$\mathrm{FJC} = \varnothing$ 的样本中仅有 [0.2]\% 最终输出正确，即缺少 FJC 几乎等价于最终做错。
Notably, only 0.65\% of samples with $\mathrm{FJC} = \varnothing$ produce a correct final output, meaning the absence of FJC is near-equivalent to an incorrect answer.

% 中文: 在上述 setting 中，四类 outcome 分别占 [XX]\% Resolved、[XX]\% Overprocessed、[XX]\% Misresolved 和 [XX]\% Unresolved，共覆盖 [XX]\% 的样本。但这套分类目前仍然是描述性的——它是否反映了真实的内部计算结构，需要因果证据来回答。
In the anchor setting, the four categories account for 78.8\% Resolved, 13.8\% Overprocessed, 5.1\% Misresolved, and 2.2\% Unresolved, covering 99.9\% of all samples (\cref{app:outcome_distribution_statistics}). However, this taxonomy remains descriptive so far---whether it reflects genuine internal computational structure requires causal evidence.

\subsection{Causal Validation}
\label{subsec:causal_validation}

% 中文: 如果 FJC 和四类 outcome 对应真实的内部结构，那么围绕它们的干预就应当产生可预测的、与 outcome 相关的效应。我们设计三组因果实验来检验这一点（详细设置、层位扫描与失败案例见 \cref{app:causal_validation_details}）。
If FJC and the four outcomes correspond to real internal structure, then interventions centered on them should produce predictable, outcome-specific effects. We design three causal experiments to test this (detailed setups, layer sweeps, and failure cases in \cref{app:causal_validation_details}).

% 中文: 在 FJC 层用答案不同但任务匹配的样本的 hidden state 替换原始 hidden state，观察最终预测是否被改写。如果 FJC 是因果特权层，patching 在此处的 answer-flip rate 应显著高于其他层。
\paragraph{Activation patching at FJC.}
Following causal localization methods \citep{meng2022locating, geiger2023causal}, we replace $\mathbf{h}^{\mathrm{FJC}}$ with the hidden state from a task-matched sample that has a different answer, and observe whether the final prediction is overwritten. If FJC is a causally privileged layer, the answer-flip rate should be substantially higher there than elsewhere. Result: the flip rate at FJC is 69.9\% ($N=150$), compared with 18.7\% at matched pre-brewing layers and 31.0\% at post-resolution layers.

% 中文: Overprocessed 的定义蕴含正确计算已形成但被后续层破坏。若如此，跳过 FJC 之后的尾部层、直接从 $\mathbf{h}^{\mathrm{FJC}}$ 解码应当恢复正确答案。
\paragraph{Layer skipping for Overprocessed.}
The definition of Overprocessed implies that a correct computation was formed but subsequently destroyed---akin to the overthinking phenomenon observed in prior work \citep{halawi2024overthinking, schuster2022confident}. If so, skipping the tail layers after FJC and decoding directly from $\mathbf{h}^{\mathrm{FJC}}$ should recover the correct answer. Result: on Overprocessed samples, skipping restores correctness in 73.3\% of cases ($N=75$); the same operation on already-Resolved samples degrades accuracy by 0\% ($N=25$)---the effect is sharply outcome-specific.
Consistent with this interpretation, late-layer hidden states also exhibit a distinct sparsity pattern. We find that Hoyer sparsity rises sharply in the final layers, and that Overprocessed samples remain more sparse than non-Overprocessed samples throughout this late-layer region (\cref{app:subsec:late_layer_sparsity_overprocessed}). This indicates that overprocessing is not only a behavioral ``right-then-wrong'' phenomenon, but is also accompanied by a measurable compression-like trend in representation geometry.

% 中文: 如果 Unresolved 意味着"没算完"而非"根本不会"，那么给模型额外的有效深度应能救回部分样本。我们将后期 hidden state 重新注入一次新的前向传播。
\paragraph{Re-injection for Unresolved.}
If Unresolved means ``not finished'' rather than ``fundamentally incapable,'' then providing the model with additional effective depth should rescue some samples. We re-inject late hidden states into a fresh forward pass; a single re-injection rescues 33.3\% of cases, and up to three re-injections rescue 46.7\% ($N=150$), indicating that a substantial fraction of Unresolved samples contain incomplete but already usable computation.

% 中文: 三组干预各自针对 taxonomy 的不同分支，均产生了与对应 outcome 一致且对其他 outcome 不一致的效应，表明 brewing-to-resolution 捕捉的是模型内部计算的真实结构而非事后归纳。
All three interventions target different branches of the taxonomy and each produces effects consistent with its corresponding outcome while inconsistent with the others, confirming that the brewing-to-resolution framework captures genuine internal computational structure rather than a post hoc narrative.
%
% 中文: 这套结构同样在模型自身的输出分布中留下了可观测信号：CSD entropy、其下降速率、以及 probe-CSD agreement 三个 GT-free 量即可在不访问 ground truth 的情况下区分四类 outcome。§4 将利用这些信号，把分析从单一 anchor setting 推广到全部六类 code primitive 的跨任务、跨模型比较。
This structure also leaves observable signatures in the model's own output distributions: the CSD entropy $H_{\mathrm{C}}^\ell$ and the probe--CSD distributional divergence---ground-truth-free quantities that achieve 87.0\% overall agreement with the true outcome labels (\cref{app:ground_truth_free_discrimination}). \S4 leverages these signals to extend the analysis from the single anchor setting to cross-task and cross-model comparisons across all six code primitives.
