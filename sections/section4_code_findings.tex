\section{What Different Code Primitives Reveal}
\label{sec:code_findings}

% 中文: Figure~\ref{fig:task_fingerprint} 把六类任务的四路 outcome 分布并排展示在同一张图中。最直接的观察是：每一种 code primitive 都诱导出自己独特的 outcome profile。这不只是"正确率不同"——accuracy 无法区分的 failure mode，在这里被拆开了。下面我们先点出各任务最有辨识度的特征，再进入三组 controlled comparison。

\subsection{Outcome Fingerprints and What They Expose}
\label{subsec:outcome_fingerprints}

\begin{figure*}[t]
\centering
\includegraphics[width=\textwidth]{task_fingerprint.png}
\caption{Four-way outcome distributions across all six task families for Qwen2.5-Coder-7B. Each code primitive induces a distinctive outcome fingerprint.}
\label{fig:task_fingerprint}
\end{figure*}

\cref{fig:task_fingerprint} displays the four-way outcome distributions of all six tasks side by side. The most immediate observation is that every code primitive induces its own distinctive outcome profile. This goes well beyond ``different accuracy''---failure modes that aggregate accuracy cannot distinguish are cleanly separated here. We first highlight the most diagnostic features of each task, then turn to three controlled comparisons.

% 中文: Copying 作为谱系中最简单的一端，以 Resolved 为绝对主导（[XX]\%），Overprocessed 和 Unresolved 均为极小的尾部。纯变量传播一旦被正确追踪，模型几乎总能把结果保持到最后。这为后续所有比较提供了 baseline。

\paragraph{Copying.}
As the simplest end of the spectrum, Copying is overwhelmingly dominated by Resolved outcomes ([XX]\%), with Overprocessed and Unresolved forming only negligible tails. Once pure variable propagation is correctly tracked, the model almost always preserves the result through to the final output. This establishes the baseline for all subsequent comparisons.

% 中文: Conditional 的 signature 以高 Misresolved 为标志（[XX]\%）。模型并不是在条件分支上"算不出来"，而是自信地收敛到了错误分支的结果。从 $H_{\mathrm{C}}^\ell$ 看，Conditional 的 Misresolved 样本往往在中后层就已经达到低熵——CSD 稳定了，只是稳定在错误答案上。这种"confident but wrong"的模式在 data flow 任务中几乎看不到。

\paragraph{Conditional.}
The hallmark of Conditional is its high Misresolved rate ([XX]\%). The model does not struggle to produce an answer for conditional branches---it converges confidently to the result of the \emph{wrong} branch. Examining $H_{\mathrm{C}}^\ell$ confirms this: Misresolved samples in Conditional often reach low entropy by the mid-to-late layers, meaning CSD has stabilized, but on an incorrect answer. This ``confident but wrong'' pattern is virtually absent in data-flow tasks.

% 中文: Short-circuit 呈现另一种 profile：Unresolved 占比显著升高（[XX]\%），同时伴有可观的 Misresolved（[XX]\%）。compound boolean 中多个 subcondition 的联合判断与 and/or 的 short-circuit semantics 给模型施加了双重压力：既要正确评估每个子条件，又要在正确位置截断 evaluation。模型经常在现有深度内无法完成这个过程。

\paragraph{Short-circuit.}
Short-circuit presents yet another profile: Unresolved rises substantially ([XX]\%), accompanied by considerable Misresolved mass ([XX]\%). The joint evaluation of multiple subconditions in compound boolean expressions and the short-circuit semantics of \texttt{and}/\texttt{or} impose a dual pressure: the model must correctly evaluate each subcondition \emph{and} truncate evaluation at the right point. The model frequently fails to complete this process within its available depth.

% 中文: Computing vs. Copying：算术改变了什么？由于 Computing 在 $\rho = 0$ 时退化为 Copying，这一对任务提供了干净的比较。fingerprint 上，Computing 的 Resolved 显著低于 Copying（[XX]\% vs. [XX]\%），而 Overprocessed 大幅上升（[XX]\% vs. [XX]\%）。算术的主要效应不是让模型"找错方向"（Misresolved 并没有显著增加），而是让已经形成的正确答案更难被保持到最终输出。brewing dynamics 支持同样的解读：相对于 Copying，Computing 的 $H_{\mathrm{C}}^\ell$ 下降更慢，$A^\ell$ agreement window 更晚且更窄，平均 $\Delta_{\mathrm{brew}}$ 为 [XX] vs. [XX] 层。随着 $\rho$ 从 0 增加到 1，这些效应单调增强（\cref{app:subsec:computing_density_sweep}）。算术延长的不是 pre-brewing，而是 brewing 本身——信息存在但尚不可用的中间状态被拉长了。

\paragraph{Computing vs.\ Copying: what does arithmetic change?}
Because Computing degenerates to Copying when $\rho = 0$, this pair provides a clean comparison. In the fingerprint, Computing shows markedly lower Resolved ([XX]\% vs.\ [XX]\%) and substantially higher Overprocessed ([XX]\% vs.\ [XX]\%). The primary effect of arithmetic is not to send the model in the wrong direction---Misresolved does not increase appreciably---but to make an already-formed correct answer harder to preserve through to the final output. Brewing dynamics support the same reading: relative to Copying, Computing exhibits slower $H_{\mathrm{C}}^\ell$ descent, a later and narrower $A^\ell$ agreement window, and a longer mean $\Delta_{\mathrm{brew}}$ of [XX] versus [XX] layers. These effects strengthen monotonically as $\rho$ increases from 0 to 1 (\cref{app:subsec:computing_density_sweep}). What arithmetic lengthens is not the pre-brewing phase but brewing itself---the intermediate state in which information is present but not yet usable is stretched out.

% 中文: Loop vs. Loop-unrolled：syntax matters. 这是全文最干净的因果对照。两类任务的底层算术完全相同（matched initial value、operation、iteration count），唯一差异是 Loop 使用 for ... in range(...) 语法，而 Loop-unrolled 把同样的计算展开为 sequential statements。如果模型只是在做 repeated addition，两者的 fingerprint 应当相近。实际上它们显著分离：Loop 的 Unresolved 明显高于 Loop-unrolled（[XX]\% vs. [XX]\%），而两者的 Overprocessed 比例相近。从 dynamics 看，Loop 的 $A^\ell$ trajectory 更脆弱，entropy collapse 更慢且更不稳定。循环理解不能被还原为等价的 sequential arithmetic——模型在处理 loop semantics 时动用了一条不同的内部路径。

\paragraph{Loop vs.\ Loop-unrolled: syntax matters.}
This is the cleanest causal contrast in the paper. The two task types share identical underlying arithmetic (matched initial value, operation, and iteration count); the sole difference is that Loop uses \texttt{for ... in range(...)} syntax while Loop-unrolled unfolds the same computation into sequential statements. If the model were merely performing repeated addition, the two fingerprints should be similar. In practice they separate sharply: Loop has significantly higher Unresolved than Loop-unrolled ([XX]\% vs.\ [XX]\%), while the two show comparable Overprocessed rates. Dynamically, Loop's $A^\ell$ trajectory is more fragile, and entropy collapse is slower and less stable. Loop comprehension cannot be reduced to equivalent sequential arithmetic---the model engages a different internal pathway when processing loop semantics.

% 中文: Data flow vs. Control flow：两种不同的瓶颈。把上述观察聚合起来，一个更广泛的 pattern 浮现了。Data flow 主导的任务（Copying、Computing、Loop-unrolled）倾向于更长的 brewing、更平滑的 entropy descent，失败时更多是 Overprocessed——瓶颈在于把正确表征稳定地保持到最后（stabilization bottleneck）。Control flow 主导的任务（Conditional、Short-circuit）则更常出现 Misresolved 和 Unresolved——瓶颈在于选择正确的执行路径并贯彻这个 commitment（commitment bottleneck）。Loop 正好位于两者之间，既有 data flow 式的长 brewing，又有 control flow 式的 Unresolved 升高，与它的 mixed 性质一致。完整的跨任务数据与 per-difficulty-level 分析见 \cref{app:subsec:per_difficulty_breakdown}。

\paragraph{Data flow vs.\ control flow: two different bottlenecks.}
Aggregating the observations above, a broader pattern emerges. Data-flow-dominant tasks (Copying, Computing, Loop-unrolled) tend toward longer brewing, smoother entropy descent, and Overprocessed as the primary failure mode---the bottleneck lies in stably preserving a correct representation through to the end (a \emph{stabilization bottleneck}). Control-flow-dominant tasks (Conditional, Short-circuit) instead exhibit elevated Misresolved and Unresolved---the bottleneck lies in selecting the correct execution path and following through on that commitment (a \emph{commitment bottleneck}). Loop sits squarely between these two regimes, displaying both the extended brewing characteristic of data flow and the elevated Unresolved characteristic of control flow, consistent with its mixed nature. Full cross-task data and per-difficulty-level breakdowns are provided in \cref{app:subsec:per_difficulty_breakdown}.


\subsection{Across Models and Scales}
\label{subsec:across_models}

% 中文: 上述 task-dependent 模式并不只属于一个模型。我们在 Qwen2.5-Coder 与 Qwen2.5（同架构，code-specialized vs. general-purpose）之间、以及 0.5B 到 14B 的规模范围内，都观察到相同的 brewing-to-resolution 结构。但 outcome 分布并不是在所有维度上同步变化——这暴露了一个清晰的 mechanism 与 capability 的分离。

The task-dependent patterns described above are not confined to a single model. We observe the same brewing-to-resolution structure across Qwen2.5-Coder and Qwen2.5 (matched architecture, code-specialized vs.\ general-purpose) and across the 0.5B--14B scale range. Yet outcome distributions do not shift uniformly along all dimensions---this exposes a clear dissociation between mechanism and capability.

Within a matched architecture, code-specialized training substantially improves the outcome distribution: the Coder model raises overall Resolved from [XX]\% to [XX]\% and reduces Unresolved from [XX]\% to [XX]\%, with the largest gains on [task placeholder]. Training changes how much the model can ultimately resolve far more than it changes where the resolution transition tends to occur.

\paragraph{Scaling.}
Larger models shift probability mass from Unresolved and Misresolved into Resolved, reflecting increased effective computational capacity. Overprocessed exhibits a noteworthy non-monotonic trend: it peaks at intermediate scales ([XX]\%) before declining---the ability to reach the correct answer emerges before the ability to stably preserve it. Meanwhile, normalized brewing duration remains stable across scales, holding at roughly [XX]\% $\pm$ [X.X]\% of total depth. Capability changes, but the computational scaffold does not.

% 中文: 跨架构。同样的 pattern 延伸到 Qwen 系列之外。在 CodeLlama / Llama 和 DeepSeek-Coder 上，outcome 分布随训练和规模移动，但 brewing 的存在和 task-dependent fingerprint 的结构保持稳健（\cref{app:subsec:cross_architecture_robustness}）。brewing 反映的是 transformer 在代码上计算的一般属性，具体的 outcome mix 则更多反映学习到的能力与有效容量。

The same pattern extends beyond the Qwen family. On CodeLlama/Llama and DeepSeek-Coder, outcome distributions shift with training and scale, but the existence of brewing and the task-dependent structure of resolution fingerprints remain robust (\cref{app:subsec:cross_architecture_robustness}). Brewing reflects a general property of how transformers compute over code; the specific outcome mix reflects learned skill and effective capacity.
