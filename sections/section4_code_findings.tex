\section{What Different Code Primitives Reveal}
\label{sec:code_findings}

% 中文: 整体 79.2% Resolved，但 aggregate 掩盖了两层差异：(1) Computing 是唯一 outlier（Resolved < 34%），(2) controlled difficulty increase 暴露各任务的 latent failure bottleneck。

\subsection{Outcome Fingerprints and What They Expose}
\label{subsec:outcome_fingerprints}

\begin{figure}[t]
\centering
\includegraphics[width=\columnwidth]{fingerprint.png}
\caption{Outcome fingerprints for Qwen2.5-Coder-7B: each task (top) flows into one of four resolution outcomes (bottom), with ribbon width proportional to sample fraction. Computing is the sole task where substantial mass flows to Misresolved and Unresolved; the other five tasks route predominantly to Resolved, with Overprocessed as the main failure channel.}
\label{fig:task_fingerprint}
\end{figure}

\cref{fig:task_fingerprint} displays the four-way outcome distributions for all six task families. The aggregate picture is dominated by Resolved outcomes (79.2\% overall), confirming that Qwen2.5-Coder-7B handles most code reasoning tasks successfully. However, this aggregate masks two layers of mechanistic diversity. First, \textbf{Computing stands out as a clear outlier}: it is the only task with Resolved below 34\%, and the only one where Misresolved and Unresolved appear in substantial proportions. Second, \textbf{controlled difficulty increases} reveal task-specific failure bottlenecks that are invisible in the aggregate fingerprints. We examine these two layers in turn.

% 中文: Copying 以 Resolved 为主（79.0%），失败全部是 Overprocessed（21.0%），无 MR 或 UR。这建立了 baseline：简单变量追踪一旦失败，总是因为后层 corrupt，而非收敛到错误答案或无法收敛。

\paragraph{Copying.}
Copying is dominated by Resolved outcomes (79.0\%), with failures consisting entirely of Overprocessed (21.0\%)---no sample is Misresolved or Unresolved. This pure-OT failure profile establishes the baseline: when the model fails on simple variable tracking, it is always because late layers corrupt an already-correct answer, never because the model converges on a wrong answer or fails to converge at all.

% 中文: Computing vs. Copying：算术改变了什么？Computing 在 ρ=0 退化为 Copying，提供 controlled comparison。Computing 是唯一 Resolved < 34% 的任务（vs. Copying 79.0%），且四种 outcome 都有实质占比。ρ sweep 显示算术不只是让任务更难，而是引发 failure mode 的质变：从 OT 主导转向 MR+UR 主导。Δ_brew 的反直觉方向（Computing 11.2 < Copying 17.9）反映的是 Computing 样本更早偏离 resolution pathway。

\paragraph{Computing vs.\ Copying: what does arithmetic change?}
Because Computing degenerates to Copying when $\rho = 0$, this pair provides a controlled comparison. Computing is the sole task with Resolved below 34\% (vs.\ 79.0\% for Copying), and uniquely exhibits all four outcome categories in substantial proportions: Overprocessed dominates at 45.9\%, but Misresolved (12.4\%) and Unresolved (8.1\%) also appear---failure modes that are \emph{entirely absent} in Copying.

A density sweep sharpens this picture. At $\rho = 0$ (pure multi-hop variable tracking, no arithmetic), Resolved is already only 40.0\%, with Overprocessed at 45.1\% and MR+UR together at 14.9\%. As $\rho$ increases to 1.0, Resolved drops to 16.4\% and MR+UR rises to 65.7\%, while Overprocessed \emph{decreases} to 17.9\% (\cref{app:subsec:computing_density_sweep}). The effect of arithmetic is therefore not merely quantitative---it triggers a qualitative shift in failure mode composition, from answer-degradation (Overprocessed) to answer-misdirection and non-convergence (Misresolved + Unresolved).

Brewing dynamics reflect the same pattern but with a noteworthy reversal: Computing's mean $\Delta_{\mathrm{brew}}$ (11.2 layers) is \emph{shorter} than Copying's (17.9 layers). This does not indicate faster resolution; rather, it reflects the fact that many Computing samples exit the brewing stage into failure---their trajectories diverge toward Misresolved or Unresolved before reaching FJC. Shorter effective brewing signals earlier departure from the resolution pathway, not swifter completion of it.

% 中文: Conditional 在 aggregate 上大部分 Resolved（82.7%），OT 是主要失败模式（11.4%），MR 仅 4.4%，UR 1.5%。"confident but wrong" 存在但不频繁。但 difficulty sweep 揭示了一个阈值效应：nesting depth 从 1 到 3，Resolved 从 98.0% 降至 62.8%，MR 从 0% 升至 11.5%。

\paragraph{Conditional.}
In aggregate, Conditional is largely Resolved (82.7\%), with Overprocessed (11.4\%) as the dominant failure mode and only modest Misresolved (4.4\%) and Unresolved (1.5\%) rates. The ``confident but wrong'' pattern---where the model selects the wrong branch yet converges stably---is present but infrequent at the task level. However, a controlled difficulty sweep reveals a sharp threshold: as nesting depth increases from 1 to 3, Resolved drops from 98.0\% to 62.8\%, and Misresolved rises from 0\% to 11.5\% (\cref{app:subsec:per_difficulty_breakdown}). Branch-selection errors are thus a \emph{latent} bottleneck that surfaces only under sufficient structural complexity.

% 中文: Short-circuit 是最稳定的 control-flow 任务：Resolved 在最高难度（3 subconditions）仍超过 81%，UR 在所有难度都是 0%。失败主要是 OT（10.4%，在 3 subconditions 时升至 15.7%），不是 MR。compound boolean evaluation 并未从根本上挑战模型的收敛能力，瓶颈在于后层保持正确答案，类似 data-flow 任务。

\paragraph{Short-circuit.}
Short-circuit is the most stable control-flow task: Resolved remains above 81\% even at the highest difficulty (3 subconditions), and Unresolved is exactly 0\% across all difficulty levels---the model always commits to an answer. When it fails, the failure is predominantly Overprocessed (10.4\% overall, rising to 15.7\% at 3 subconditions), not Misresolved. This suggests that compound boolean evaluation, despite requiring joint assessment of multiple subconditions, does not fundamentally challenge the model's ability to converge; the bottleneck is preserving a correct answer through late layers, similar to data-flow tasks.

% 中文: Loop vs. Loop-unrolled：syntax matters. 最干净的因果对照——相同底层算术，仅 syntax 不同。Aggregate 上两者接近（Loop 84.8% vs. Loop-unrolled 89.3%），但 difficulty gradient 暴露质的差异：Loop 在 iteration=2 出现 U 形谷底（56.8% Resolved），然后恢复到 97.1%；Loop-unrolled 单调下降，worst 在 iteration=4（65.5%）。

\paragraph{Loop vs.\ Loop-unrolled: syntax matters.}
This pair provides the cleanest causal contrast: identical arithmetic (matched initial value, operation, and iteration count), differing only in whether the computation is expressed as a \texttt{for} loop or as unrolled sequential statements. In the aggregate fingerprint, the two tasks are similar---Loop 84.8\% Resolved vs.\ Loop-unrolled 89.3\%---and neither shows substantial Misresolved or Unresolved.

The critical difference emerges in the difficulty gradient. Loop exhibits a striking non-monotonic pattern: Resolved drops to 56.8\% at iteration${=}2$ (with Overprocessed spiking to 36.4\%) before recovering to 97.1\% at iteration${=}4$. Loop-unrolled, in contrast, degrades monotonically, reaching its worst point at iteration${=}4$ (65.5\% Resolved). The inverted difficulty curves suggest that the model processes loop syntax through a qualitatively different internal pathway. One possible explanation is that iteration${=}2$ falls in a worst-case regime: the loop body executes enough times to require genuine iterative tracking (unlike iteration${=}1$, which reduces to a single pass), but not enough to establish a repetitive pattern that the model can exploit (as may happen at iteration${=}4$).

% 中文: 难度作为机制多样性的透镜。六个任务在 aggregate 层面的主要失败模式都是 OT（后层 corruption）。任务身份本身在 aggregate 水平上并不能尖锐地分离失败 profile。差异在 controlled difficulty increase 下才显现，每个任务揭示出不同的失败轨迹。

\paragraph{Difficulty as the lens for mechanistic diversity.}
Across all six tasks, the aggregate failure mode is predominantly Overprocessed---late-layer corruption of a once-correct answer. Task identity alone does not sharply separate failure profiles at the aggregate level. The differentiation appears under controlled difficulty increases, where each task reveals a distinct failure trajectory:
\begin{itemize}[nosep]
\item \textbf{Computing} ($\rho$ sweep): qualitative shift from OT to MR+UR---multi-hop tracking is the primary bottleneck, arithmetic amplifies it into a different failure regime.
\item \textbf{Conditional} (depth 1$\to$3): Misresolved emerges as a latent bottleneck at high nesting---branch-selection errors surface only under structural complexity.
\item \textbf{Loop} (iteration sweep): non-monotonic U-shape reveals a syntax-specific fragility absent in the unrolled equivalent.
\item \textbf{Short-circuit} (conditions 1$\to$3): remains OT-dominant even at high difficulty, behaving more like a data-flow task.
\end{itemize}
Full per-difficulty breakdowns are in \cref{app:subsec:per_difficulty_breakdown}.


\subsection{Across Models and Scales}
\label{subsec:across_models}

% 中文: 上述 task-dependent 模式并不只属于一个模型。我们在 Qwen2.5-Coder 与 Qwen2.5（同架构，code-specialized vs. general-purpose）之间、以及 0.5B 到 14B 的规模范围内，都观察到相同的 brewing-to-resolution 结构。但 outcome 分布并不是在所有维度上同步变化——这暴露了一个清晰的 mechanism 与 capability 的分离。

\begin{figure*}[t]
\centering
\includegraphics[width=\textwidth]{brewing_stability.png}
\caption{Brewing structure across scaling, training, and architecture. (a) KDE of normalized First Joint Correct Layer (FJC) positions. Within the Qwen family, FJC positions remain closely aligned across scale and training, while other architectures show a shifted but still coherent transition depth. (b) Violin plots of normalized brewing duration, which varies less than overall outcome quality across the same comparisons.}
\label{fig:brewing_stability}
\end{figure*}

The Computing outlier and the difficulty-dependent failure differentiation described above are not confined to a single model. We observe the same brewing-to-resolution structure across Qwen2.5-Coder and Qwen2.5 (matched architecture, code-specialized vs.\ general-purpose) and across the 0.5B--14B scale range. Yet outcome distributions do not shift uniformly along all dimensions---this exposes a clear dissociation between mechanism and capability.

% 中文: Coder vs. Base. 在 Computing 上，Coder 把 Resolved 从 20.5% 提升到 33.5%，OT 从 64.1% 降到 45.9%。但 FJC 归一化位置高度相关（r=0.90），平均偏移仅 0.6%。训练改变的是 capability，不是 mechanism。

\paragraph{Coder vs.\ Base.}
Within a matched architecture, code-specialized training substantially improves the outcome distribution: on Computing, the Coder model raises Resolved from 20.5\% to 33.5\% and reduces Overprocessed from 64.1\% to 45.9\%, primarily by improving the model's ability to preserve correct answers through late layers. Yet at the task level, the normalized FJC positions remain highly correlated between the two models (Pearson $r = 0.90$, $p = 0.014$, $n{=}6$), with a mean shift of only 0.6\% of total depth (\cref{fig:brewing_stability}). Training changes how much the model can ultimately resolve far more than it changes where the resolution transition tends to occur.

% 中文: Scaling. 更大模型把 UR/MR 转向 Resolved。Computing 上 OT 非单调——中等规模峰值后下降。归一化 brewing duration 跨规模稳定（~40%）。

\paragraph{Scaling.}
Larger models shift probability mass from Unresolved and Misresolved into Resolved, reflecting increased effective computational capacity. In particular, the Unresolved rate drops most sharply with scale, suggesting that additional depth provides the computational headroom needed for convergence even on difficult instances. On Computing, Overprocessed exhibits a noteworthy non-monotonic trend, peaking at intermediate scales before declining---the ability to reach the correct answer emerges before the ability to stably preserve it. Meanwhile, normalized brewing duration remains stable across scales (\cref{fig:brewing_stability}). Capability changes, but the computational scaffold does not.

% 中文: 跨架构。同样的 pattern 延伸到 Qwen 系列之外。在 CodeLlama / Llama 和 DeepSeek-Coder 上，outcome 分布随训练和规模移动，但 brewing 的存在和 task-dependent fingerprint 的结构保持稳健（\cref{app:subsec:cross_architecture_robustness}）。brewing 反映的是 transformer 在代码上计算的一般属性，具体的 outcome mix 则更多反映学习到的能力与有效容量。

The same pattern extends beyond the Qwen family. On CodeLlama/Llama and DeepSeek-Coder, outcome distributions shift with training and scale, but the existence of brewing and the task-dependent structure of resolution fingerprints remain robust (\cref{app:subsec:cross_architecture_robustness}). Brewing reflects a general property of how transformers compute over code; the specific outcome mix reflects learned skill and effective capacity.
